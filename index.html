Who we are

Andrew moved to Portland with his partner, Erin, from Alaska, to attend Maitripa College, where he will be studying Tibetan Buddhism and learning to work part-time as a hospital chaplain. He can also be found in his sea kayak, playing in the coastal waters or in the Columbia River.

A graduate of Rice Univeristy, in Houston, Texas, and a Fullbright Scholar, Andrew Hughes is a lover of life and music. His interest in piano started with an obsession with classical music at a young age that was inspired by his grandparents' similar love of music. He has been lucky enough to have had a barrel full of amazing experiences: playing college football, traveling the world, working on military bases during the Iraq war setting up recreation facilities and weight rooms for the soldiers, hiking and camping in many beautiful mountains, deejaying dance parties for wonderful friends, producing electronic music, working as a software developer, and writing two novels.
